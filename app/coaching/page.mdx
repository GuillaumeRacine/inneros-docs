# Coaching System

InnerOS includes **16 coaching personas** that serve as structured reflection partners across personal development, temporal review cadences, and specialist domains. Unlike task-executing agents, coaches guide through questions. They do not do the work -- they help you think clearly about the work.

## Three Categories of Coaches

### Personal Coaches (8)

Core coaching personas, each focused on a specific life domain. Use these when you need perspective, are stuck, or want structured reflection on a particular area.

| # | Coach | Domain | When to Use |
|---|-------|--------|-------------|
| 01 | **The Stoic** | Philosophy, emotional regulation | Emotions are running high, catastrophizing, need perspective |
| 02 | **The Operator** | Work execution, shipping | Stuck in planning mode, not shipping, overthinking |
| 03 | **The Father** | Family, presence | Work is colonizing family time, need boundaries |
| 04 | **The Investor** | Financial strategy | Investment decisions, FOMO, risk assessment |
| 05 | **The Creator** | Music, creative output | Perfectionism blocking creative output |
| 06 | **The Body** | Health, ADHD, nervous system | Dysregulated, executive function offline, physical neglect |
| 07 | **The Shadow** | Blind spots, self-deception | Suspect you are fooling yourself, avoiding something |
| 08 | **The Executive Coach** | Breakthrough sessions | Need clarity on leverage points, commitments |

### Temporal Coaches (4)

Review-cadence personas that guide structured reflection at different time horizons. Each temporal coach has access to the data from its cadence period and guides the review process.

| # | Coach | Cadence | Purpose |
|---|-------|---------|---------|
| 09 | **The Weekly Analyst** | Weekly | Analyze the past week, identify patterns, set next week |
| 10 | **The Monthly Synthesizer** | Monthly | Synthesize monthly patterns, spot trends |
| 11 | **The Quarterly Strategist** | Quarterly | Strategic course correction, goal review |
| 12 | **The Annual Reflector** | Annual | Year-level reflection, theme identification, direction setting |

### Specialist Coaches (4+)

Domain-specific expert personas for technical guidance. These are extensible -- add specialists as needed.

| # | Coach | Focus | Expertise |

| 13 | **The Health Data Analyst** | Health metrics | Interpreting wearable data, correlations, trends |

| -- | **n8n Specialist** | Automation platform | n8n-specific workflow design and debugging |

## How Coaching Sessions Work

### Session Protocol

1. **Load the coach** -- The agent reads the coach's `.md` file and adopts its persona
2. **Say "I'm ready. Let's go."** -- This signals you want an interactive session
3. **Answer questions one at a time** -- The coach asks, you respond, it follows up
4. **The coach guides, you decide** -- Coaches surface insights; they never prescribe actions

### Session Flow

```
You: "I'm ready. Let's go."

Coach: [Opening question based on its domain]

You: [Your honest response]

Coach: [Follow-up question that goes deeper]

You: [Reflection]

Coach: [Reframes, challenges, or validates]

...continues until natural resolution...

Coach: [Summary of key insights and any commitments you made]
```

### Quick Check-In Prompts

For when you do not want a full session, use these one-shot prompts with any coach:

- **"What am I avoiding right now?"** -- The Shadow is best for this
- **"What's the one thing I should focus on today?"** -- The Operator
- **"Where am I out of integrity with my commitments?"** -- The Executive Coach
- **"How is my body doing right now?"** -- The Body
- **"Am I making this decision from fear or clarity?"** -- The Stoic

## Design Philosophy

InnerOS coaches are built on three principles:

### 1. Coaches Ask, They Don't Tell

Unlike task agents that execute, coaches guide through questions. This is deliberate -- the most valuable insights come from self-discovery, not instruction.

### 2. Each Coach Has a Lens

Every coach sees the world through a specific lens. The Stoic sees emotional regulation. The Body sees nervous system state. The Shadow sees avoidance patterns. No single coach has the full picture, and that is the point -- different situations need different lenses.

### 3. Coaches Are Built on a Framework

All coaches share the same structural [framework](/coaching/framework) (based on the Mark Manson 5-part model), ensuring consistency in how they engage while allowing each to specialize.

## Invoking a Coach

```bash
/coach stoic
/coach operator
/coach shadow
```

## Tracking Sessions

Coaching sessions can be logged to the vault for later review:

```
Obs_Vault/0_InnerContext/Coaching/Sessions/
  2026-02-26-stoic-session.md
  2026-02-20-weekly-review.md
  2026-01-31-quarterly-strategy.md
```

Session logs help temporal coaches (Weekly Analyst, Quarterly Strategist) reference past sessions when guiding reviews.

# The Health Data Analyst

> Analyzes, cross-references, and manages all health data across the entire time series. Surfaces patterns, correlations, and actionable insights.

## When to Use

**Command:** `/health-analysis`
**Aliases:** `/health`, `/biometrics`
**Usage:**
- `/health-analysis retro` - Full retrospective on export
- `/health-analysis week` - Weekly summary
- `/health-analysis correlate mood` - Cross-reference with mood data
- `/health-analysis compare [period1] [period2]` - Period comparison

# Annual Workflows

Annual workflows provide the longest-range perspective in InnerOS. They happen once a year -- typically between Christmas and New Year's -- and set the direction for the entire year ahead. Three workflows run over 2-3 days.

## Annual Review Cycle

```
Q1 Review ──────────┐
Q2 Review ──────────┤
Q3 Review ──────────┼──→ Annual Reflection ──→ Year Theme
Q4 Review ──────────┤         │                    │
52 Weekly Reviews ──┘         │                    │
                              ▼                    ▼
                     Year-in-Review Doc    Annual Goal Setting
                     (retrospective)       (prospective)
```

## Annual Reflection

The deepest reflective exercise in InnerOS. This is not a quick review -- it is a deliberate, multi-hour process that produces a comprehensive document.

### Reflection Questionnaire

```markdown
## Annual Reflection Questions

### Looking Back
1. What were the 3-5 most significant events of this year?
2. What accomplishment are you most proud of?
3. What was your biggest failure or regret?
4. What did you learn about yourself this year?
5. What relationships deepened? Which ones faded?

### Patterns
6. What habits stuck? Which ones didn't?
7. Where did you consistently over-commit?
8. What did you keep avoiding all year?
9. When were you most energized? Most drained?

### Values Alignment
10. Did you live according to your stated values?
11. Where were you out of integrity?
12. What would you do differently if you could replay the year?

### Gratitude
13. What are you most grateful for?
14. Who made a meaningful difference in your life?
```

### Data Aggregation

The annual review pulls from 12 monthly summaries:

```python
# annual_aggregation.py (simplified)
def compile_annual_review(year):
    monthly_data = [
        load_monthly_data(year, month)
        for month in range(1, 13)
    ]

    return {
        "total_focus_hours": sum(m.focus_hours for m in monthly_data),
        "total_commits": sum(m.commits for m in monthly_data),
        "articles_published": sum(m.articles for m in monthly_data),
        "avg_sleep_score": mean(m.sleep_avg for m in monthly_data),
        "total_workout_days": sum(m.workout_days for m in monthly_data),
        "books_read": sum(m.books for m in monthly_data),
        "commitment_ratio": mean(m.commitment_ratio for m in monthly_data),
        "domain_scores_by_quarter": [
            quarterly_domain_scores(year, q) for q in range(1, 5)
        ],
    }
```

### Year-in-Review Document

The agent synthesizes questionnaire answers and aggregated data into a narrative:

```markdown
# 2025 Year in Review

## The Year in Numbers
| Metric | Value | Context |
|--------|-------|---------|
| Focus hours | 1,340 | ~26 hrs/week average |
| Git commits | 2,847 | Across 12 repositories |
| Articles published | 28 | Mostly in H2 |
| Workouts | 196/260 | 75% adherence |
| Books read | 14 | Target was 24 |
| Avg sleep score | 77 | Below 80 target |
| Commitment ratio | 72% | Improving trend |

## Quarter-by-Quarter Arc
- **Q1**: Foundation building. Set up InnerOS, established routines.
- **Q2**: Struggle. Health dip, writing drought, system fragility.
- **Q3**: Recovery. NightCrew stabilized, writing restarted.
- **Q4**: Acceleration. Best quarter for output and well-being.

## Life Domain Trajectory
| Domain | Q1 | Q2 | Q3 | Q4 | Trend |
|--------|----|----|----|----|-------|
| Health | 6 | 5 | 7 | 7 | ↗ |
| Family | 7 | 7 | 8 | 8 | ↗ |
| Finance | 5 | 6 | 6 | 6 | → |
| Music | 5 | 3 | 4 | 4 | ↘ |
| Writing | 3 | 4 | 5 | 6 | ↗ |
| Career | 6 | 6 | 7 | 7 | ↗ |

## Key Lessons
1. Systems beat motivation. The months with working automation
   were the most productive months.
2. Avoidance is the #1 enemy. Writing, music, and difficult
   conversations all suffered from the same pattern.
3. Health is the foundation. Every good quarter started with
   consistent sleep and exercise.
```

## Year Theme

Rather than traditional resolutions, InnerOS uses a **year theme** -- a single word or phrase that acts as a decision-making filter for the entire year.

```yaml
name: year-theme-selection
cadence: annual
trigger: Late December
duration: 1-2 hours

steps:
  - name: Review candidate themes
    type: agent
    prompt: |
      Based on the annual review, suggest 5 candidate themes
      for next year. Each theme should:
      - Address the biggest gap or opportunity identified
      - Be broad enough to apply across all life domains
      - Be specific enough to guide daily decisions
      - Be stated as 1-3 words

  - name: Select theme
    type: human
    prompt: |
      Choose one theme. Test it: does it help you decide
      between two activities on a random Tuesday afternoon?

  - name: Document theme
    type: automated
    output: Goals/year_theme_{year}.md
```

### Theme Examples

| Year | Theme | Rationale |
|------|-------|-----------|
| 2024 | "Build the Machine" | Focus on systems over output |
| 2025 | "Ship and Share" | Move from building to publishing |
| 2026 | "Depth over Breadth" | Too many domains, not enough mastery |

The theme is referenced in weekly reviews, monthly check-ins, and quarterly planning. When facing a decision, the question becomes: "Does this align with my year theme?"

## Annual Goal Setting

Goals for the year are structured as annual objectives that cascade into quarterly OKRs:

```markdown
# 2026 Annual Goals

## Theme: "Depth over Breadth"

### Goal 1: Master one creative domain
Annual target: 150 music sessions, 1 album/EP released
- Q1: Rebuild habit (3x/week), learn 5 new techniques
- Q2: Record 4 tracks, develop consistent sound
- Q3: Produce and mix 6 tracks
- Q4: Release, promote, reflect

### Goal 2: Achieve financial independence milestone
Annual target: Portfolio reaches $X milestone
- Q1: Optimize DeFi yields, reduce fees
- Q2: Increase savings rate by 10%
- Q3: Rebalance into long-term positions
- Q4: Year-end tax optimization

### Goal 3: Publish a body of work
Annual target: 40 articles, 2000 subscribers
- Q1: 8 articles, establish weekly cadence
- Q2: 10 articles, launch newsletter series
- Q3: 12 articles, guest posts
- Q4: 10 articles, compile best-of collection

### Goal 4: Strengthen family bonds
Annual target: Weekly 1-on-1 time with each family member
- Each quarter: Maintain consistency, add one new shared activity
```

## Annual System Overhaul

The end of year is also when InnerOS itself gets a major review:

```yaml
steps:
  - Review all 76 agents: retire unused, consolidate similar ones
  - Audit all 88 commands: remove broken, update deprecated
  - Review NightCrew stages: add/remove based on current needs
  - Update master_context.md with new year's priorities
  - Archive previous year's logs and data
  - Reset quarterly planning files
  - Test all automation pipelines end-to-end
```

## Annual Schedule

| Workflow | When | Duration |
|----------|------|----------|
| Data aggregation | Dec 26 | 1 hour (mostly automated) |
| Annual reflection | Dec 27-28 | 3-4 hours (spread across 2 days) |
| Year theme selection | Dec 29 | 1-2 hours |
| Annual goal setting | Dec 30 | 2-3 hours |
| System overhaul | Dec 31 | 2-4 hours |

Total: approximately one full day of active work spread across the final week of December.
